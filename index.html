Troubleshooting Tips

Even a premium VPN can sometimes encounter glitches. Below are common problems and how to fix them — without needing to call a support number.

🔹 Can’t connect to a server: Switch to a different server location. Sometimes certain servers are under maintenance. Also, try switching between UDP and TCP protocols in the app settings.
🔹 Slow speeds: Choose a server closer to your physical location. Disable any other VPN or proxy services. Also, check your base internet speed without the VPN to rule out ISP issues.
🔹 VPN keeps disconnecting: Enable the “Network Lock” kill switch. If you’re on Wi‑Fi, try switching to a wired connection or changing your DNS settings to Google (8.8.8.8) or Cloudflare (1.1.1.1).
🔹 Streaming service blocked: Use a server that is optimised for streaming. ExpressVPN has dedicated IPs for some platforms — check the “Smart Location” feature. Clear your browser cache and cookies as well.
🔹 App not opening / crashing: Restart your device. Reinstall the ExpressVPN app (your settings are saved). Make sure your OS is up to date.
🔹 Login issues: Clear your browser’s cache and cookies if logging in via the web. For mobile apps, force‑close and reopen. Ensure your subscription is active (check your email for renewal notices).
